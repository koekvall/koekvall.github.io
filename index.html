About

I am a postdoctoral researcher in statistics at Karolinska Institute. My research interests include mixed models, likelihood-based inference in non-standard settings, dimension reduction, and computational statistics.

Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at TU Wien and completed a Ph.D. in statistics at the University of Minnesota in 2019.
